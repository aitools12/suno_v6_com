Overview

What Suno V6 is used for

Suno V6 is an AI music generator for users who want a shorter path from idea or lyrics to usable audio output. The strongest public use cases are text-to-music generation, lyrics-to-song conversion, browser preview, and export-ready audio creation.

Workflow

How Suno V6 works

1

Write a prompt or paste lyrics

2

3

Refine and export

Adjust the prompt, compare versions, and export the strongest result for your project.
